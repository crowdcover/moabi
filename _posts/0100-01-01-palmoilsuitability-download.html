International Institute for  Applied Systems Analysis (IIASA) products – Terms of Use and User Agreement

This document describes the terms and conditions of access to the data of the IIASA-study Global oil palm suitability assessment (in the following referred to as “the map”) for the sole purpose of academic research and publication.

Access to and use of the map by the undersigned is licensed by IIASA-ESM subject to the following principles and conditions:

  • The current and all future versions of the map remain the exclusive property of IIASA-ESM. No work performed on or with the map transfers any claim of ownership to any other user.
  • The map is made available only for your personal use. In case one of your collaborators wishes to access the map, IIASA-ESM needs to be informed and he/she needs to agree to and submit this user charter before accessing it.
  • You may modify and combine the map as required for your needs. However, you must report these changes to the IIASA-ESM to make sure the changes can still be compatible with the principles of the methodology adopted for computation the map.
  • You are encouraged to publish using the map in any kind of format including scientific articles, reports, posters, PowerPoint presentations, etc., provided that you:
    • Acknowledge that the map was developed initially at IIASA-ESM and cite the IIASA interim report IR-15-006 as source. Please cite the report as

      • Pirker, Johannes, and Aline Mosnier. 2015. Global Oil Palm Suitability Assessment. IIASA Interim Report 15-006. Laxenburg, Austria. http://www.iiasa.ac.at/publication/more_IR-15-006.php.

    • offer the authors of the map the possibility to review the reports/articles using the map before submission for publication.
  • Any use of the map beyond the scope or duration of the above-mentioned purpose is possible only after written approval by IIASA-ESM in the form of an update to these terms and conditions.
